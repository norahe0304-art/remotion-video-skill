# Narrative Templates by Industry

Each template defines: **scene combo**, **UI mockup types**, **headline patterns**, **proof strategy**, and **tone**. These are starting points — ALWAYS adapt to the user's specific story.

**Usage:** After scraping (Step 1), match the brand to the closest industry template. Use it as a scaffold, then customize based on user's Step 1.5 answers.

## How to Choose

| Brand signals | Template |
|---------------|----------|
| "AI", "agent", "intelligence", "automate" | AI SaaS |
| "payments", "banking", "financial", "treasury" | FinTech |
| "deploy", "infrastructure", "CLI", "SDK", "API" | DevTool |
| "store", "sell", "merchant", "commerce" | E-Commerce |
| "patient", "clinical", "HIPAA", "health" | Healthcare |
| "security", "protect", "threat", "vulnerability" | Cybersecurity |
| "team", "collaborate", "workspace", "project" | Collaboration |

If the brand doesn't fit neatly, **combine elements** from two templates. E.g., an AI DevTool = AI SaaS tone + DevTool UI mockups.

## Anti-patterns

- **Don't copy headlines verbatim.** These are PATTERNS to adapt, not scripts.
- **Don't force all 3 showcase vignettes.** 2 strong ones > 3 weak ones.
- **Don't ignore the user's story.** Templates are STARTING POINTS, not final answers.
- **Don't mix tones.** A cybersecurity video should NOT feel "playful."
